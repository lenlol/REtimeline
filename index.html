a condensed timeline of the events of the
survival horror video game franchise

1967: Progenitor is discovered from the Sonnetreppe or "Stairway of the Sun" flower which is found in Kijuju, Africa. Edward Ashford intends for Progenitor to be a cure for paralysis.

November 10, 1967: Lisa Trevor, the daughter of the Spencer mansion architect, is injected with Progenitor.

1968: Umbrella Corporation is founded by Ozwell E. Spencer, Edward Ashford and James Marcus. Spencer and Marcus fully realize. that Progenitor can be used for biochemical warfare after Ashford's death.

1969-1970: Alexander Ashford constructs the Antarctic Base.

1971: The Ashford twins, Alexia and Alfred, are born.

September 19, 1977: Marcus creates the T-Virus, or Tyrant, by combining Progenitor with leech DNA.

1977: Albert Wesker begins his life with the Umbrella Corporation.

1981: The first zombies are created. Birkin also creates the "hunter" strain using the T-Virus.

1981: At age ten, Alexia Ashford is appointed senior researcher at the Antarctic Base where she begins development on T-Veronica.

1982: T-Veronica enters human testing and ends in 1983 when Alexia injects herself with the T-Veronica virus.

1988: T-Virus is completed and James Marcus is assassinated upon its completion. "Nemesis" is created as a result of the completed T-Virus.

1991: The potential of "Nemesis" is fully realized and the Nemesis Project is created. It is also discovered that Lisa Trevor is the key to the G-Virus, which is discovered by William Birkin.

1996: Albert Wesker establishes S.T.A.R.S. (Special Tactics and Rescue Service).

1997: Barry Burton (Ex-SWAT) and Chris Redfield (Ex-Air Force) join S.T.A.R.S.

May 11, 1998: James Marcus is able to revive himself from his T-Virus research with his queen leech. He seeks revenge and infects the Spencer mansion.

June 16, 1998: The Raccoon Weekly warns the public to stay away from Arklay Mountain after reports of rabid wild dog packs surface.

July 23, 1998: The events of Resident Evil Zero take place in the Arklay Mountains, centered around S.T.A.R.S. Medic Rebecca Chambers and Billy Coen, a former U.S. Marine.

July 24, 1998: The events of Resident Evil take place in the Arklay Mountains and the Spencer Mansion, centered around Jill Valentine, Chris Redfield, Barry Burton and Rebecca Chambers.

September 20, 1998: Umbrella operative Hunk assassinates William Birkin. Birkin injects. himself with the G-Virus in order to survive. As a result the city's water supply is infected with the T-Virus.

September 24, 1998: The infection begins to manifest itself.

September 28, 1998: The events of Resident Evil 3: Nemesis begin in Raccoon City, centered around Jill Valentine.

September 29, 1998: The events of Resident Evil 2 begin in Raccoon City, centered around rookie cop Leon S. Kennedy, Claire Redfield, Sherry Birkin and Ada Wong.

October 1, 1998: The events of Resident Evil 3: Nemesis come to an end with Jill Valentine and Carlos Oliveira escaping Raccoon City before it is cleansed by a nuclear missile. Umbrella releases public story as the meltdown of a reactor destroying Raccoon City.

Fall 1998: Post-Raccoon City incident Jill Valentine and Chris Redfield found the B.S.A.A. (Bioterrorism Security Assessment Alliance) which is founded with the help of TRICELL.

December 27, 1998: The events of Resident Evil: Code Veronica begin on Rockfort Island and are centered around Claire Redfield and Steve Burnside.

2003: Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine discover Umbrella's involvement in a Russian outbreak; they also discover that Albert Wesker survived his "death" in the events at the Spencer mansion.

2004: Umbrella Corporation is shut down due to Chris and Jill's exposure.

2004: The events of Resident Evil 4 take place in Spain and are centered around the now-U.S. Government agent Leon Kennedy. The cult Los Illuminados. discovered the parasite strain Las Plagas underneath the old Salazar castle.

2005: The events of CGI movie Resident Evil: Degeneration take place in Harvardville, centered around Leon S. Kennedy and Claire Redfield, now Terrasave employee. Wilphar WilPharma, the company responsible for creating the T-Virus vaccine, is exposed for their infection of remote cities to bolster their vaccine sales. TRICELL buys out WilPharma when the company fails and with the company obtains samples of the T-Virus and the G-Virus.

2005: The events of Resident Evil: Revelations take place, centered around Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine. The game ends with Jill and Chris returning to the Spencer Mansion in time to witness Albert Wester murder Ozwell E. Spencer. Jill sacrifices herself to take down Wesker and save Chris.

2009: The events of Resident Evil 5 take place centered around Chris Redfield in Kijuju, Africa, and Sheva Alomar. The Mijini are infected with a similar Las Plagas to Resident Evil 4. The same flower used to created Progenitor is used to create a new strain called Uroboros. Albert Wesker is killed by Chris Redfield in a volcano.

December 24, 2012: The events of Resident Evil 6 begin, centered around three storylines. The first revolves around Leon S. Kennedy and his new partner Helena Harper. Another involves Chris Redfield and his new partner Kenne Piers. The third storyline follows Jake Muller and Sherry. Birkin, who is now a D.S.O agent.
